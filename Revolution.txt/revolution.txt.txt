        COMBINATION THERAPY PROTOCOL: OSIMERTINIB + PEMETREXED
                  "THE PHOENIX PROTOCOL"
================================================================================

BACKGROUND:
-----------
Osimertinib is the standard-of-care for EGFR-mutated NSCLC, but resistance inevitably develops. This Phase II study investigates if combining it with Pemetrexed chemotherapy can deepen response and delay resistance.

HYPOTHESIS:
-----------
The combination of a 3rd-generation EGFR TKI (Osimertinib) with a folate antimetabolite (Pemetrexed) may:
1. Eradicate persister cells that survive initial TKI therapy.
2. Enhance chemosensitivity in an EGFR-suppressed environment.
3. Achieve a higher objective response rate (>75%).
